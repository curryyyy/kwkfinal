PROTECTING NATURE

Enlite was created by four young women, Elizabeth, Kerrine, Audrey, and Lydia at a Kode with Klossy camp.

We noticed the numerous environmental problems around us, and wanted to combine our skills of coding with the environment to advocate for change. Our topics include: pollution, endangered animals, and misleading / greenwashing brands.

Our mission: to inform others about environmental and animal-related issues and provide ways people can help animals and the Earth.
